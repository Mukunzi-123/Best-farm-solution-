Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our smart farming solutions.

How do I set up my first farm monitoring system?

Setting up your farm monitoring system is straightforward. First, contact our team to schedule a free site assessment. We'll analyze your farm layout, crop types, and specific needs. Then we'll create a customized sensor placement plan and configure your dashboard for optimal monitoring. Our technicians will handle the installation and provide comprehensive training.

What types of crops can be monitored with your system?

Our system supports monitoring for all major crop types including grains (wheat, corn, rice), vegetables (tomatoes, lettuce, peppers), fruits (apples, grapes, berries), and specialty crops (herbs, flowers). The system automatically adjusts monitoring parameters, alert thresholds, and recommendations based on your specific crop selection and growth stages.

Is there a mobile app available for monitoring?

Yes! Our comprehensive mobile app is available for both iOS and Android devices. You can monitor all your farm metrics in real-time, receive instant alerts and notifications, access detailed analytics and reports, control irrigation systems remotely, and even capture field photos for documentation. The app works offline and syncs when connectivity is restored.

What kind of support is included with my subscription?

All subscriptions include 24/7 technical support, regular system updates, and access to our online knowledge base. Premium plans include priority support, dedicated account management, on-site visits, and personalized training sessions. We also provide seasonal optimization consultations to help you maximize your system's effectiveness throughout the growing season.
