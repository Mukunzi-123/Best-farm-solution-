Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up my first farm monitoring system?

Setting up your farm monitoring system is easy. First, contact our team to schedule a site assessment. We'll help you determine the best sensor placement and configure your dashboard for optimal monitoring.

What types of crops can be monitored?

Our system supports monitoring for all major crop types including grains, vegetables, fruits, and specialty crops. The system automatically adjusts monitoring parameters based on your crop selection.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes! Our mobile app is available for both iOS and Android devices. You can monitor your farm, receive alerts, and access analytics from anywhere.
